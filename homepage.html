Financial History

Users can view a history of their expenses, showing the timestamp, category, and amount. Entries can be deleted individually, providing control over the expense history. Users can also view a history of their savings, displaying the timestamp and amount. Individual savings as well as expense entries can be deleted for better management.

A visually appealing and easy-to-understand interface helps users track their financial situation.

Dashboard

Users are greeted with a dashboard displaying their financial overview:

  • Total Savings
  • Total Expenses
  • Remaining Income
  • Overall Income

A visually appealing and easy-to-understand interface helps users track their financial situation.

Quick Visual

Quick visualizations are provided through real-time timeline and bar charts, illustrating savings amounts by months. Additionally, a real-time chart showcases expense categories by amounts, offering insights into spending and savings trends.

A visually appealing and easy-to-understand interface helps users track their financial situation.

Add Income

Income seamlessly integrates, enhancing your financial standing. The dashboard offers a clear view, with the option to manage individual entries for accuracy.

Add Expenses

Users can add expenses by selecting a category (food, grocery, fuel, bills, or other) and entering the amount. If an invalid amount or category is added, an apology is shown. The expense is categorized and reflected in the total expenses on the dashboard.

A visually appealing and easy-to-understand interface helps users track their financial situation.

Add Savings

Savings can be effortlessly recorded by specifying the desired allocation, ensuring financial goals are met. Real-time visualization through dynamic graphs provides a clear overview of your progress, and individual savings can be conveniently removed or adjusted to maintain accurate financial records.

A visually appealing and easy-to-understand interface helps users track their financial situation.
